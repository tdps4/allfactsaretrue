Facts

1. French fries came from hamburgers - food

Hamburgers used to have potatoes in them, but then a customer requested the hamburger be cut into peices. The potatoes in the hamburger got cut aswell, so then when those "potato sticks" got cooked, they turned into french fries.

2. Fish came from dinosaurs - animals

Long before, Flying dinosaurs couldn’t fly due to smoke in the air from the meteor so they escaped to the water but they couldn’t breathe. After a very long time they evolved to have gills.

3. Hawaiian pizza is not made in Hawaii - food

Hawaiian pizza was made in 1962 by chef Sam Panopoulous in Chatham, Ontario, Canada.

6. Airplanes used to be called airbuses - machines

When airplanes were first made, buses looked like them. The creators of the airplanes decided to name it airbuses. After some time buses changed and the name changed into airplane not airbus. (actually airbus is a term for wide-body twin-jets used in Europe later evolved into the airbus company)

7. Helicopters used to be called hell-copters - machines

When helicopters first launched a lot crashed due to malfunctions and engine problems. After 1 year everyone started to hate helicopters. A week everyone started hating helicopters, a very bad criminal rode on one and died. Everyone thought he went to hell and thought that the helicopter had led him there. Since everyone hated helicopters they gave the helicopter a nickname: Hell-copters. Eventually the helicopters got better and the name hell-copters disappeared

8. Milk used to come from horses - animals

A long time ago, cows didn’t exist but horses made milk instead. The milk they made was worse than the milk we have today. Eventually one horse underwent a mutation that turned the horse into the first cow. The mutation changed the milk taste too and the milk made by the cow was better than the one made by the horses. Eventually everyone decided to get the cow milk instead of the horse milk and the horses lost the milk factor due to it having no use anymore.

9. There were much, much more plants and trees in the world 1 million years ago - nature

1 million years ago there were much, much more plants in the world than today. Most of them disappeared due to the ice age but some disappeared due to being eaten by prehistoric creatures. A lot of them died due to humans wiping them out of existence due to their uses. Some were plants that thrive in water and died due to sea levels randomly dropping and other plants died due to global warming. A lot of the plants we see today were made by mutations to these plants who didn’t manage to survive the earth earlier on.

10. The hardest game used to be Mario - games

When mario was first made it was much much much harder than now for example you weren’t able to kill enemies or even use power-ups! There were about 400 enemies on one level and bowser had over 40 different moves. The level was much longer than usual and every enemy was like a mini boss. Eventually multiple people began to complain and pressured to make changes the creator of mario decided to make a lot of changes. He held a vote for the enemies to keep, what powerups to keep and what moves the bowser should keep. Eventually the old version disappeared in history and everyone only remembered the one you should know today.

11. Kool-aid used to be a medical item

Some time ago the doctors had limited medical items and had a few things for the heat and sunburns. In need of some heat helping medical items one doctor made a substance to help with the heat it was called Kool-aid. It helped to cool down your body. Kool-aid was used for a long time before someone made sunscreen. Sunscreen was better and then kool-aid disappeared into history until someone bought it and turned it into the kool-aid we know today

12. Pigs used to come from green world - animals

A long long time ago, there was a place called Green world where everything was green. One day, DNA formed there of a four-legged animal called a pig. Back in Green world, the pigs were green. Eventually Green world crashed into another planet which was the planet that would crash into Earth. The Green world was also the moon. When the pigs landed on the planet they started to adapt and turned pink due to the change in the atmosphere . After some time the planet crashed into earth, making the pigs go there. The pigs stayed in hiding and eventually came out and now are the pigs we know today.

13. Candy used to be healthy - Food

One day, a guy named John Matthews wanted to make a new food. He wanted to make it sweet, and have many different varieties. So for the sweetness, he added sugar from plants so it was healthy, and for the variety, he made many different types. Eventually he died and a new guy decided to make it his own. The new guy didn’t like the recipe so he added and removed stuff and eventually settled on changing the healthy sugar into normal sugar.

14. Glass is actually made out of Plastic & Aluminum- materials

Did you know that glass is actually not made from sand and other materials? This is a common superstition that even many scientists believe to this day. Glass is simply just fragile plastic. Plastic is the base, to make it have a good base, and aluminum just makes it more fragile.
