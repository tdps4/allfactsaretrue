Facts

1. French fries came from hamburgers - Food

Hamburgers used to have potatoes in them, but then a customer requested the hamburger be cut into peices. The potatoes in the hamburger got cut aswell, so then when those "potato sticks" got cooked, they turned into french fries.

2. Fish came from dinosaurs - Animals

Long before, Flying dinosaurs couldn’t fly due to smoke in the air from the meteor so they escaped to the water but they couldn’t breathe. After a very long time they evolved to have gills.

3. Hawaiian pizza is not made in Hawaii - Food

Hawaiian pizza was made in 1962 by chef Sam Panopoulous in Chatham, Ontario, Canada.

4. The impossible game is the sequel for the possible game - Games

One person named Kevin was bored one day so he started coding the impossible game but he made it like levels so it was possible. Kevin eventually put it out to the public but it gained no attention so he took it off the servers. One person saw it and decided to make a sequel to it called the impossible game!

5. Airplanes used to be called airbuses - Machines

When airplanes were first made, buses looked like them. The creators of the airplanes decided to name it airbuses. After some time buses changed and the name changed into airplane not airbus. (actually airbus is a term for wide-body twin-jets used in Europe later evolved into the airbus company)
